faq.txt

common questions · updated quarterly
What does a DanDomain → Shopify migration cost?

Quoted per project after a paid discovery call. Typical catalog of 1–10k SKUs with two integrations and a redirect map under 5,000 URLs runs €8,000–€18,000 ex VAT. Larger B2B builds with Business Central or e-conomic wiring run higher. No hourly billing — fixed scope, fixed price.

How do you preserve SEO equity across a platform change?

Three things on the same day: a complete 301 redirect map matched URL-for-URL to the old site, the new shop's structured data matched to the old schema, and a crawl diff run by an independent tool within 48h of cutover. Most "lost ranking" stories come from skipping one of these.

Can you also build on WordPress?

Yes. WordPress + WooCommerce is about a third of the practice. Same workflow — fixed scope, weekly demos, post-launch stabilisation. WP makes more sense than Shopify when content is the product, full data ownership matters, or the catalog model breaks Shopify's variant ceiling.

How fast can a WP site actually load?

Most WP sites I ship hit under 1.0s First Contentful Paint on a cold cache. Page cache + object cache + edge fragment cache + OPcache + critical CSS + a strict plugin diet. Speed is a budget, enforced in CI.

Will I be one client among many?

No. Independent practice. Capacity capped at two simultaneous migrations. You work with me directly, every week, single thread for questions, Friday demo. When the project ends you own the code, the runbook, and the keys.
