FAQ

Q1DanDomain → Shopify timeline?
1–6 weeks. 1,000-product shop usually 2–3 weeks. Fixed timeline up front.
Q2Will SEO rankings survive?
URL-for-URL 301 map, preserved meta/schema, ported sitemap. Rankings hold or recover in 30–60 days.
Q3WP → Shopify with custom plugins?
Mapped to Shopify apps, metafields, Functions or theme code. Variable products → variants. Blog moves with redirects.
Q4How fast can my WP site go?
Sub-1s FCP. Page + object (Redis) + fragment + edge (Cloudflare) cache. OPcache, critical CSS, lazy-load.
Q5Keep e-conomic / Business Central?
Yes. Direct app where it exists, custom connector otherwise. Klaviyo, GLS/PostNord, payments re-wired.
