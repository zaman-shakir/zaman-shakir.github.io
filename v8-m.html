Pre-flight questions.

The five things every merchant asks before takeoff.

What does a DanDomain → Shopify migration cost?

Quoted per project after a paid discovery call. Typical catalog of 1–10k SKUs with two integrations and redirect map under 5,000 URLs runs €8,000–€18,000 ex VAT. Fixed scope, fixed price, no hourly billing.

How do you preserve SEO equity across a platform change?

Three things on the same day: complete 301 map URL-for-URL, new shop's structured data matched to old schema, crawl diff within 48h. Skip one and rankings drop. I don't skip.

Can you also build on WordPress?

Yes. WordPress + WooCommerce is about a third of the practice. Same fixed-scope workflow. WP wins when content is the product or the catalog model breaks Shopify's variant ceiling.

How fast can a WP site actually load?

Most ship under 1.0s First Contentful Paint on a cold cache. Page + object + edge cache, OPcache JIT, critical CSS inlined, strict plugin diet. Speed is a budget, enforced in CI.

Will I be one client among many?

No. Capacity capped at two simultaneous migrations. Direct contact, weekly demos, Friday calls. When the project lands you own the code, the keys, and the runbook.
